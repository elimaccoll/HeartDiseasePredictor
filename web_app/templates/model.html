Modeling

We wanted to create a basic model to predict whether an individual is prone to heart disease depending on multiple attributes. To do this, we evaluated 5 different classifiers to determine which produced the best results with our dataset and used it for creating the model that is implemented on the website. We found that the logistic regression classification technique achieved the highest accuracy.
